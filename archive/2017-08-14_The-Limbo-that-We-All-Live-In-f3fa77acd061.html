The Limbo that We All Live In

Welcome to Limbo

Our reality is filtered through the perception of our senses. Let’s just assume that most humans have five senses.

These five senses are accepted as touch, smell, hearing, sight, and taste. Each sensation and observation sparks a path of neurons within our brain.

Each experience calls up previous experience and new thoughts. We may even perform an action as a result.

We never truly know if it will achieve the desired effect depending on the action we perform.

You can buy a house that you find suitable. It could burn down the next day.

You can date someone. They could be the best or the worst.

Each action has its corresponding reaction within the world. The reaction is never 100% guaranteed.

This leads to the infinite limbo that we live in. The number of uncertainties you will face will always over weigh the certainties that you have.

Something can not be a certainty unless we know all the variables or factors involved.

Since do not know all the factors or variables that contribute to the universe, we are all open to uncertainty.

If we did know all the variables, there would be no need to experiment or do science.

How can we escape this limbo?

You can’t really. It has always existed since the beginning and end of time.

However,

You can learn to accept the uncertainty and push through obstacles that you may face.

This limbo is something that we must all face at one point or another.

Why not stop trying?

That is definitely one option.

This is really is a personal thing. What do you want to look back upon when close to your own death?

That is the question that only you can truly answer.

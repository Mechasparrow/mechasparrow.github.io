Ate a Vegan Dinner Today

Went out and ate Vegan.

Just came back from a Hammercircuit session today. After some work in the studio we went out to a vegan dinner. I guess I’ll make a food comparison and call it a day.

How does it compare to non-vegan food?

It is not that different from eating food that was non-vegan. The main difference was that they replace the cheese and meat.

Other then that the food tasted just as good or even better then if it contained animal product. This is most evident with the vegan fried ice cream that was consumed.

The Vegan Fried Ice Cream

It is possible to created non dairy ice cream. As an extension of this you can make vegan fried ice cream.

I have to say the ice cream we had was great. This is taking into account there was no dairy milk as well. It tasted just as sweet as regular fried ice cream would be.

Conclusion

Note: I don’t normally eat vegan everyday. It is a goal of mine of course.

The dinner I had proved that there is great tasting food without any animal products. It is really a matter of keeping it varied for every meal.

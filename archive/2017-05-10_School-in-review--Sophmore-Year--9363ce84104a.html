School in review (Sophmore Year)

Hey everyone, back with another blog post. With summer school closing in, I’d like to talk about this school year in review. NOTE: life piece

VE (Spire)

One of the courses that helped change my outlook on business was Virtual Enterprise. In class, I was able to

  • build powerful connections,
  • learn valuable business skills
  • gain new friends.

I also learned how well connected a business has to be to succeed. This is important to know considering how businesses have to evolve in response to technology and trends.

Relationships

This year of high school, I gained a better view of what I find important in a relationship and friendships. I have 2 years of high school left.

I’ve concluded that there is no reason to fret about who I hang out with.

Work and Projects

The competition project I worked was Morality Machine. It was for the GlobalHack V competition. I learned new methods for building and prototyping software. I also learned what goes into running a team. I can’t wait for the next GlobalHack or hackathon event.

I have worked on several side projects this year. I planned to build an application 2 every month. This led to some useful and not-so-useful applications. The best one so far would have to be Emojilog.

It will be interesting to see what applications I will build for the following school year.

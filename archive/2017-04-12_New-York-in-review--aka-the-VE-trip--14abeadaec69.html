New York in review (aka the VE trip)

Hello, everybody. I finally made it back to St. Louis! Last Week, I wrote two articles during my New York trip. This one and this one.

So if you want to know more details, feel free to read those two.

I’d like to review the trip on a day by day basis i.e Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday.

Monday

On Monday, We had the business plan competition. This was in the Microsoft business building close to Times Square.

Our team at SPIRE was able to score pretty at around top 16 . This is out of 500+ firms in the United States alone.

We had some trouble with the projector. Luckily, since I was there, I was able to fix them up to the projector.

Afterward, we went back to the hotel and chilled for a bit. When we finished that, we started working out logistics for tomorrow’s Trade show.

Our booth turned out pretty good. It had a quick tear down and build up speed.

We went to Central Park. one of the coolest parks I’ve been to. It reminded me of Forest Park in St. Louis, but with more fields.

Tuesday

On Tuesday, we had the trades fair. This entailed selling Snap-chat stickers and Ad Space on the VE portal.

I actually got to do sales for the first time. , I could write a whole article on how awesome it was. It felt like an awesome accomplishment to sell something to someone.

We made several successful sales. Near the end of the afternoon, we packed up and left for the hotel.

Wednesday

This was a Tuesday for 80% of the day. The other 20% was dinner at a fancy Italian restaurant,tour bus, and VE Gala.

Tour bus did not show anything too special. Yet, we did get some good views of some buildings

Only the participants of the VE Business Plan Competition were able to attend. Our CEO also participated in the Venture Challenge.

She succeeded and won $10,000 and office space for one year.

The dinner was nice though. Not as cool as the other thing.

Thursday

On Thursday, we went on full out tourist mode. We visited Wall-street in the morning, and visited We-work.

The coolest part of Wall-street was checking out the Bull Statue and Fearless girl.

We-work was like a different office on every single floor.

We pretty much chilled at the hotel after that.

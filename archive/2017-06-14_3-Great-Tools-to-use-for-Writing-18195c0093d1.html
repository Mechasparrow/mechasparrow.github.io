3 Great Tools to use for Writing

Hey, everyone. It’s Michael. I’ve been writing for several months now. Over time, my writing style and workflow has changed. In this post, I would like to share the tools that I use in my writing workflow.

These tools can be used for any type of writing you are trying to do. They can also be chained together.

Mindmaps (XMind)

I use personally use XMind for creating my mind maps. I am a large fan of mind maps. They can help you flesh out a single concept into a series of diverse and deep topics.

Grammarly

Grammarly makes sure to fix any grammar errors in your post so you don’t have to. That way when your writing you can focus on the content instead of the semantics.

Hemmingway

Hemmingway is the last part of my writing process. Hemmingway helps you increase the readability of your writing piece. This helps make sure that your article is not super confusing and understandable.

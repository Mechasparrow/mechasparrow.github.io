Introducing IT2GO2

IT2GO2 is a technology company that offers a unique combination of IT and Digital Marketing services.

We have the technology and social media expertise in order to assist small business owners and individuals. The founders of IT2GO2 are Michael Raber and Viktoria Vartanesova.

Our goals are to provide business solutions to maximize the effectiveness of your marketing campaigns and visibility on the internet. We offer meaningful and creative marketing solutions for your small business or company. We always try to research our client’s market to better help them success.

Who are we?

Michael Raber has worked at AT&T for 18 years. He is also has worked in network administration, network architecture, computer setup, and computer repair. He is currently in the process of being certified for digital forensic recovery.

Viktoria Vartanesova has worked in the marketing field for over 10 years. She has worked at Charter Media for years supporting the advertising department, as well as providing digital marketing consulting for the past few years as a business consultant. She has helped startups develop businesses by guiding them on what steps should be taken in order to increase their outreach on the internet through Social Media Marketing.

We hope that you will consider our services for your business to help gain an advantage in your market.

The Hammercircuit has awoken

Ever pondered life? Thought there was more to just the daily gruesome world that is life? Pestered with distractions it may seem difficult to break out and find your own path.

Well, that’s what Hammercircuit has gone out to do. We try to provide powerful ideas that go outside the confines that society has pulled us into.

We strive to help others think beyond the common perspective that has been ingrained into our minds by societal influences. Our mantra is driven by multifarious philosophies, life questions, and geopolitical matters.

We work towards a media network that distributes these ideas.

This is accomplished through podcasts, blog posts, and videos.

Who created this and how is this special?

The founding fathers of the Hammercircuit media network are both teenagers. This provides a fresh perspective on old and new issues that impact ourselves and the world.

Michael Navazhylau, a.k.a the Mechasparrow, is a computer programmer, entrepreneur, and self-proclaimed tech mage.

Carter Henry (Überlegen Auge), an engineer in training, experimental blacksmith, writer, and the visionary spiritual Übermensch.

Together, they collaborate to bring their backgrounds together to form unique viewpoints on life, philosophy, and political matters. Because of their combined experience, they are able to form unbiased, fair statements in business and current events.

Together, they have awoken the Hammercircuit, an idiosyncratic creation forged of metal, technology, and wit.

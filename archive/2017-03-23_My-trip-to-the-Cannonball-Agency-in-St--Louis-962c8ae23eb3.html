My trip to the Cannonball Agency in St. Louis

On Wednesday, the Spire team and I were able to take a trip to Cannonball’s office in St. Louis.

Once we were there, they were able to give us a tour of their office. Not only that, but they also sat down with us and gave us useful advice for our virtual firm.

If you don’t know the virtual firm I’m referring to, I’m talking about SPIRE. SPIRE is a virtual marketing firm for the Virtual Enterprise network.

I’ll just cover some key points during our conversation with the ad agency.

Every part of the team is crucial.

Cannonball was able to show us how important cross-functionality between departments are. Without the necessary constraints and feedback, a consulting company can easily run off the tracks.

Teamwork and Communication.

During our discussion, the Cannonball team emphasized how important teamwork and communication is. It didn’t matter what phase of a project they were in.

Throughout its projects, Cannonball regularly checks in with clients, so that everyone is up to date.

When a project starts, there is often a brainstorming session with a portion of the creative team. Designs go through several iterations and critiques from the team.

Connections are key.

When they were starting out, the company had very few clients. Since they were very loyal to their clients and performed good work, they slowly grew their network.

This goes for anything with business. Always take the chance to network. You never now if a connection you make will lead to a new opportunity.

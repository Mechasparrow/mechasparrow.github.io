2016 review and plans for the future

Wow 2016 is almost over. What a great feeling it is to look back and see so many milestones. I’d like to take the time in this article to discuss future plans for my writing and software projects.

2016 in a nutshell

Software

I’ve made 546 contributions on github compared to my 131 in 2015. That’s almost 4 times more software. What I can see from this year is that I have put myself in more situations in which I have to build solutions and solve problems. I obtained my Full stack certificate from Lynda.com in early August. I have revamped my website 2 times. With the 2nd one being fairly flexible for modification. You can visit it here at http://mechasparrow.github.io/. I can’t wait to see what new projects I will tackle during the next year.

Business

I attended the Virtual Enterprise class. In this class, I learned and improved on several skills, such as resiliency and communication. When I think back to what I seen in the class. There is a clear divide between the doers and the leaders. The leaders will face adversity without visible fear. While the doers will begin to look towards the leaders when some obstacle faces them. This is just a generalization and does apply to all the people in the class. My favorite part was the Tennessee trip. We met several people and discovered new things that we have not previously know about one another.

Writing

I tried writing daily blogs, but as the blog progressed I realized that in order to get the most out of my writing I needed to give times towards proper planning and editing of my writing. Currently my blogs are written every 1–2 weeks. I hope to drop this down to 2 times a week.

Plans for 2017

The plans will be discussed in 3 parts software, business, writing/content. Here are some general goals for 2017

  • Become a more philosophically enlightened person
  • Establish new connections and start using social media in a positive manner
  • Make school work for me and not the other way around. (Take spark, AP Comp Sci etc)

Software

I hope to write even more code then last year. I will do this by establishing to main side projects each month. If a side project needs a month long extension, then I add it to the next month, but keep it where I am only working on 2 side projects at a time. I will also try to reach to other developers and trainees by posting tutorials and youtube videos.

Business

I will continue taking the VE class. Because my VE firm is of the top 20 in the nation, we are able to the Youth Business Summit in New York. There I will meet students from all over the world. I will have to complete the firm website for the website competition. I plan to help introduce VR and Drones as new platforms for the VE world.

Writing/Content

For writing and content. I wish to do these things

  • Get blogs down to 2 times a week
  • Write tutorials and engaging content for beginning developers
  • Create a youtube channel and give technical talks and tutorials
  • Begin creating video games as a options 1 month side project

To summarize I believe spreading content among several platforms helps create greater out reach.

I hope you liked this 2016 postmortem and 2017 future article. If you like my content, feel free to share and follow. Have something to say? Comment in the comments section below.

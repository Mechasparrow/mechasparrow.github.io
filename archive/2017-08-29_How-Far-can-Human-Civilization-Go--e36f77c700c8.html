How Far can Human Civilization Go?

How far?

This piece is largely philosophical and based off a discussion I had with a friend of mine.

After recording another episode of the Hammercircuit talkshow/podcast, the question of human potential arose.

How far can human civilization go?

This can be split even further, but let’s assume this has to do with human existence and progress. Each year, we innovate and solve problems that affect us, humans.

However, will there ever be a point where progress stops? A point where we can no longer fight entropy and humans as we know it snuffs out?

The average human lifespan is 79 years give or take. This means that thinking beyond 100 years is a stretch.

Life itself is wired to survive and usually, it finds a way.

Will humans be able to survive every situation it encounters? Well, we can’t really know since we can not prepare for every possible event that may or may not occur.

How far can human civilization go?

As far as we want to take it.

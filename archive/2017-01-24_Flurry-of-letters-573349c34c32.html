Flurry of letters

NOTE: This is a opinion piece

Last weekend I received a large sum of letters from colleges. At first this seems great, but after you get to the third letter you realize that there is not much that the colleges do to differentiate themselves. Most colleges will just send a template letter linking you to special information about choosing colleges. This as at least what I received.

What really matters when it comes to selecting college come down to ROI, College Life, and Location.

ROI

The amount of money you will be able to make compared to the amount of money that you had to pay the college for the degree.

College Life

How is the college community. What is the quality of the campus, food, lecture halls, and clubs? Its often 4 years of your life. You don’t want it to be completely terrible.

Location

Where is it located? Do you need it close to home? Then you probably wont be looking at a college that’s far a way. Do you need it to be a temperate climate? Then you probably won’t go to a college in Boston or Seattle.

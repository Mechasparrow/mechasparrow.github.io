Started ACT Prep

I started taking ACT prep classes this week. The schedule and classes they are giving us for the next 3 weeks should prepare us. I have expectations for the class. Let’s go through a few of them.

Example Problems

Problems should be taken from previous versions of the ACT. This will help students have a good idea of what to expect. This will also help them develop test taking strategies.

Pacing

The ACT test is timed. Because each section is timed differently, the pacing is crucial. I would like to see how it is applied in the Reading and English section.

General Test Taking Strategies

Each test has its own set of tricks. The ACT is no different. By knowing the ins and outs of the test format and strategy, the test becomes less intimidating.

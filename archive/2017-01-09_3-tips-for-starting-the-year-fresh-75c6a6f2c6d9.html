3 tips for starting the year fresh

Going to start my second semester of the high school year. There are some ways to make second semester run way smoother then how the first might have gone down. Here are some tips for starting the year fresh and ready.

1. Use a task management tool

Often in high school we are tasked with several assignments from multiple classes. Sometimes a task management or project management tool can be of some use. Here’s some software you could possible use

  • Cybernotes
  • Trello
  • Kanbanchi

Each of these have their own benefits and downsides. Use whatever suits you. Cybernotes is more aimed for note taking but It can also be used for keeping track of tasks that are assigned.

2. Do things ahead of time

When your assigned a lengthy project such as type x in y days, try to complete it in 1/2 or 3/4 the time given. This give you time to look over your project and get feedback on it. It helps prevent procrastination in addition to that. If you can find a way to trick yourself into thinking its due a lot earlier than it actually is then you can help defeat that procrastination and in turn produce high quality work. It also might give you more down time.

3. Find a study partner or study group

Do you even find yourself not always understanding the material in class? It might help to have a study partner or study group to help go over things that you didn’t quite get, but others in your group might have. Say your awesome at math, but sometimes you have trouble in science, but you have a friend who is a science wiz and has trouble at math. You two could help each other out and become study partners!

With these tips, I hope your next year in high school or college might go smoother. If you liked the read please be sure to follow, like and share the article.

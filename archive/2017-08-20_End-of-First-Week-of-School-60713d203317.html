1 week down. Many more that follow.

Hey everyone. It is that mechanical sparrow. This week was the first week of high school for me as a Junior. My schedule is pretty sweet this year as well.

I guess I’ll give an overview of some stuff that’s going on.

Crime and Law

I have an interest in social sciences along with government. I saw crime and law as an option for a history course and I decided to take it.

The teacher is quite nice and knowledgeable.

I honestly can’t wait to learn more about the legal system.

Spark

Spark is the rest of my classes after Math, Science, English, and Crime and Law.

I’m currently in the Spark tech strand. This week in Spark has been mostly foundation stuff and preparing for what’s to come.

All my classmates in the strand with me are really cool and interesting people. I look forward to working with them this year.

On Commitment

Let’s talk a bit about commitments

Let’s talk about commitment. Taking the responsibility to do a task or a series of tasks at a repeated interval. So let’s talk about the aspects of commitments

Responsibility

Each commitment requires shouldering the responsibility that comes with it. You have to remember to do the tasks. You got to schedule the tasks. It becomes a constant.

Time Allocation

There is a limited amount of time in a single day. It is up to us humans to balance our commitments with our lives.

Staying Reliable

When you make a commitment, it comes with a level of trust as well. This is especially true when it is with other people. It is best to make a commitment that you can keep.

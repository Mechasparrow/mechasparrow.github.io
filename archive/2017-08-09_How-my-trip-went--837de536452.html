Hey, everyone. I just returned from my trip to California the other day. I’d like to talk about how it went a bit.

Here we go.

Overview

I stayed in Cali for about 2 weeks give or take. We were in staying in the Bay Area to visit some friends.

We visited several places across the Bay Area such as Silicon Valley, San Francisco, and Cupertino.

The Vlog

The moment the trip began, I started vlogging. There were several motivators to try this out. Honestly, it was to get better at vlogging and be more comfortable talking to a camera. You can check out the vlog on this playlist.

Computer Museum

The best part of the vacation for sure was the computer museum. It showed the history of computing since its very beginning.

I’ve gained a strong interest in history over the years, so the museum was very interesting to me.

The suburbs are not that special

The bay area itself is not that special. It is the people and companies who live there that really make up Silicon Valley.

There are quite a bit of mountains

I was able to hike in the mountains next to San Francisco. The views that you can get sometimes are just breathtaking.

I would recommend hiking next to shore if you get the chance.

San Francisco

During my visit, I was able to check out San Francisco. We took a train to get there from where we were staying.

There we checked out the piers, parks, and cliffs. Not only that, we also checked out the notorious Golden Gate Bridge.

When we were viewing it, we were not able to see much. This was because it was San Francisco is perpetually foggy.

Conclusion

The trip I took was not the worse but certainly not the best. It had its ups and downs, but I am happy that I was able to take the trip.

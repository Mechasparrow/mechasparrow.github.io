Dev log #22 — Vacation Edition

This dev log will be a special edition. This is because I am currently on vacation. So I’ll go over what I have been doing during the trip.

The main three this week are CommStart, vlogging, and blogging.

P.S Sorry this is not on Thursday as usual

CommStart

A couple days back I released v0.1.8 of CommStart. I also published it on Product Hunt.

CommStart is a web application that helps you start conversations. Right now, it generates a topic and pulls information from Wikipedia about that topic.

Sadly, it was not as polished nor as feature rich as I wanted. This was because I was pressed for time.

That’s alright because I will be releasing the next version once I return.

You can check out CommStart here

Vlogging

When I first arrived for my trip, I decided to start recording for some reason.

After looking at the raw footage, I realized that I could edit them into a sort of story. I also started vlogging to get better at video editing.

I will stop vlogging once I return home. This will be most likely replaced by dev vlogs.

Blogging

For August, I have challenged myself to blog for 30 consecutive days. This is to build up self-discipline and consistency.

I hoped you liked this week’s dev log. If you did, please be sure to like, share, and follow.

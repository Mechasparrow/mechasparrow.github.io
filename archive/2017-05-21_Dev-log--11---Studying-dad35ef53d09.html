Swipe-file

I’m not exactly sure if I have talked about this idea before. It goes a little like this.

Ever found something that is interesting and you want to reference to it later for a specific topic? Well, that’s the goal of swipe-file *.

A swipe file is known as a collection of text and templates to go off of. Its usually used by writers.

The main goal for swipe-file is to help organize useful information.

This could be akin to google but like a personal search engine. Users could search for useful and non-useful articles and information that they found. This could also help users who get their news from a variety of sources.

This will most likely be a full-stack application.

*The name is temporary by the way.

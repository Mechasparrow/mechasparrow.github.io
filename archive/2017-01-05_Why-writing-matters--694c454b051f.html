Why writing matters.

In November 2016, the search for fake news spiked. This was during the United States Presidential election. It was mainly the result of news sites propagating false information through Facebook and other social media sites.

Why this matters

72% of American adults who use the internet use Facebook. This means that almost 3/4 of the online American voters could have been effected by the fake news on Facebook and possibly other social or news websites.

What this means

It has become easier to spread information good and bad across the world through the internet. It is our responsibility as writers and users of this global network to provide information that is accurate and correct in spite of the overwhelming amount of negative and incorrect news written.

What can you do?

Writing is a double edged sword. On one side it can spread propaganda, misinformation, and prejudice, but on the other it can spread truth, justice, and hope. we can cross check our information and other news sources in order to validate news. Which side of the sword will you choose to wield?

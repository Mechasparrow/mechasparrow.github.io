Awesome resources for design

I’ve been designing quite a bit in my internship and VE class. Not only that but I often design mock ups of my apps before building it. So I’d like to share some of the resources that go into my design process.

Colors

Colors help add flair and life to your designs. Here are the tools I use for this.

  • Color palette generator: https://coolors.co/
  • Material Color tool: https://material.io/color
  • Color wheels https://color.adobe.com

Graphics

For graphic creation such as layouts, flyers, logos, etc, I use the following tools.

  • Online graphic creation: https://www.figma.com
  • Offline (Scalar) graphic creation: https://inkscape.org/en/
  • Offline (Rasterize) graphic creation and photo editing: https://www.gimp.org/

Well, that’s about it when it comes to the tools in my workflow. Hope you found these resources useful. If so, please like, follow, and share.

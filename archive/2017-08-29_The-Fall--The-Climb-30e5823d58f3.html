The Fall, The Climb

As I fall…

Here I was. On top of the cliff. With no one for miles around. It was lonely and I was alone.

And with loneliness comes pain and sadness.

This was the top.

I look below and see the past that has built this cliff. I look up and see nothing but blue skies.

The loneliness was enough. It was time to fall…

I close my eyes and take a leap of faith not knowing what will await me in the fog below.

As I fall, the memories and pains of the past pass in front of my eyes.

The very memories I had to sacrifice. The very pain I had to endure. To get to the top of the cliff.

As I fall, loneliness leaks away.

As I fall, the joy begins to soak in.

The very structure I trapped myself in was slowly crumbling away.

As I got closer to the ground below, my fall slowed and I landed on my feet.

I looked behind me to find the cliff was no more.

No structure,

No past,

No obligation.

Yet even on ground zero, there is a loneliness.

And with loneliness comes pain and sadness.

It is time to climb once again.

Starting a programming club at my High School

Yesterday, my team and I went through our on-boarding session for hackClub. It went pretty smoothly, and I’m pretty excited for it.

Why I’m starting a programming club

I’m starting a programming club because I feel that programming is an art form that goes beyond the textbook. Computer Science taught by my high school teaches the bare bones.

With this club, I hope to teach students through fun projects instead of a textbook. We will also be attending programming competitions such as GlobalHack and CodeDay.

What am I teaching?

I will help students learn how to build web applications and video games. I honestly can’t wait to see what students will build.

That’s really about it for now. The first club meeting will be in about 2 weeks. If you liked the short little post, please be sure to like, follow, and share.

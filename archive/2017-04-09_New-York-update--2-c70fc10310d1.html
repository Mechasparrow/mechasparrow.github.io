New York update #2

Today is the last day of my trip, so I would like to talk about some of the things that we did today.

This would include visiting the NASDAQ center in Times Square, 9/11 memorial, Wall street, WeWork, and Top of the Rock.

It was quite a jam-packed day, so let us get into it.

NASDAQ Center

We woke up early in the morning to see the opening of the NASDAQ trading. It was Parkinson’s foundation that opened that day.

There was quite a bit of hollering and whistling when it opened for trading. We even got our picture taken in front of the NASDAQ podium.

Wall Street

In Wall Street, we took a tour through the financial district. This included visiting the New York Museum of Finance, and the Bull Statue along with the Fearless Girl.

We learned quite a bit about the history of American finance. Close to Wall Street was the 9/11 memorial.

9/11 memorial

A couple friends and I decided to visit the memorial.

At first, we saw remains of the destroyed towers but later on, we watched and listened to some heart-breaking recordings of the event.

There was so much to take away from the memorial and new things were learned.

For example, 9/11 was actually not the first time the World Trade Center was attacked. It was also targeted by terrorists in the 90s as well.

WeWork

The CEO of SPIRE, Liza Tarakanova was able to win the VE venture challenge competition. This gives her office space at WeWork, a co-working office in New York and several other cities.

To get a better feel for where Liza will be working, we decided to give the office a visit. Big thanks to Nick Chapman for giving us a tour of the space.

Each floor has its own theme. There are also several additional perks that the office provides based off their plan.

It has given me a new perspective on office space.

Top of the Rock

Top of the Rock is basically a place in New York where you can get a nice view of the city skyline.

I did take some pictures. I’ll make sure to include below so you can take a look for yourself.

So that was the end of the 2nd New York trip update. I will be in St. Louis again by the time I publish this post. Next week I will be writing a piece about the trip overall rather than just the segments.

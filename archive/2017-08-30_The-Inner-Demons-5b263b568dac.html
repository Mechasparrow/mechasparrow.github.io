The Inner Demons

inner demons

Those demons they beckon to me. They know my name.

When did they come? They seemed like they were always there.

Often times the demons will be silent. Other times they will be screeching.

Screeching to be heard. Demanding to be acknowledged.

Each a manifestation of a specific trait of mine. These are my demons.

Yet…

These demons are my friends.

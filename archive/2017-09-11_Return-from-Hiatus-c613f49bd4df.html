Return from Hiatus

The Mechasparrow has returned.

Over the past week, I have refrained from writing.

I spent August blogging daily. I was not used to this. When I completed the last piece, it felt almost as if I had run out of words.

Since I have returned, it may be good to say what my new blogging schedule will be.

The New Schedule

The pieces from my blog will arrive on Sundays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays.

Each day will cover a specific theme. The themes are Philosophy, Technical, and Life.

Philosophy and Thought Pieces

I often spend too much time thinking. Sometimes my thoughts actually sound good on paper. That’s what the philosophy and thought pieces are about.

They will often take a vague question or an overlooked topic and drill down into it.

Much of these pieces will be shaped by my experiences and perspectives on philosophy.

Technical

These are beneficial posts in the fields of business, engineering, design, etc. This will include lists, tutorials, and general advice.

Life

Life is the wildcard of the mix. This is typically dev logs. Dev logs are posts that discuss what I have been working on for the week.

In addition to dev logs, this will include creative writings such as poems, short stories, and narrative essays.

Why creative writing?

I made the stunning realization that I am kind of creative during my 1-month daily blogging adventure )

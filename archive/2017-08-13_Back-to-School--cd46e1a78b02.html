Back to School…

Mechasparrow’s high school adventure begins once more

Hey, everyone. It is Mechasparrow. I have school starting again next week. It’s actually going to be really interesting this year.

Why? Because only half of it is actually going to be outside of the high school.

What do I mean? Well, I’ll tell you.

Spark! — The program where you don’t listen to teachers lecture you

For the last 2 years of High School, I will be taking a experiential school program called Spark! Spark! is different then traditional high school because it is based on the student’s ambitions and interests.

Rather than being told what to do, you do what you want to do and explore.

This is done by learning by experience instead of listening to lectures of a teacher.

Sounds like Study Hall (or Anarchy), What’s the catch?

Well, it is not without some conditions and guidance. Though they help enrich the experience rather than obstruct it.

The student has to do something though :D

The program is split into six strands or branches.

The six strands are Incubator, Health Sciences, Teaching and Learning, Technology Solutions, Engineering, and Bio-science.

Each strand gives the student real world experience that they would not normally have in high school.

I’m taking the Technology Solutions strand. If you have been following this blog for awhile, you might know that I enjoy writing software from time to time.

Also as I wrote in a previous article, I find high school monotonous. Spark! will balance it out for me I guess.

What about your high school classes?

Oh, that? Yeah, I’m doing it what I need to make it work. I still have Math, Science, English, and History. After that, it is straight to Spark!

3 Ways to Battle Burnout

Over the past few days, I have been having some difficulty with writing and programming. I felt as if I did not have the energy nor resources necessary to create.

In the midst of this feeling, I figured out how to escape the black hole of burnout.

This is achieved through the usage of one of the three methods I will be describing. There are definitely other methods to combat burnout than just the ones I will be discussing.

Method 1: Exercise!

Exercise has been know to provide many healthful benefits to the body and mind. It can help relieve stress, improve memory, and boost your overall mood.

What I first realized is that I may be suffering from withdrawal from exercise. During the beginning of summer, I established a morning workout routine to start the day.

This would provide a good boost for the rest of the day’s events. Since I am currently on vacation, I had to adopt a different plan, so now I try to swim daily.

Method 2: Practice alternative creative tasks and/or hobbies

Alternative activities give the mind rest from the monotony that may be your main job or task. It also activates different parts of the brain that you would otherwise not use in your daily job.

After programming almost daily during this summer, I realized that it could not be the only thing I should do. That’s why I began writing. It can also be included that I began creating YouTube videos as well.

Method 3: Put yourself out of your comfort zone.

Doing something you are not comfortable with creates incentive and reward. There is a level of uncertainty and mystery introduced in the situation you are in.

In the end, it jolts your mind out of automatic thinking and thought patterns by introducing new elements and perspectives.

Conclusion

The mind is one of the most interesting parts of the human body. Like any part of the body, it must be maintained and checked.

Hopefully the methods discussed above will be of some use to you next time you may experience burnout.

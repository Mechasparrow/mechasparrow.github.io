Fashion Adventures at the St. Louis Art Museum

Yesterday, I went to the St. Louis Art Museum. They were holding a special exhibit called Reigning Men.

It showed the progression of menswear through 1715 to 2015.

There were five room that covered different aspects of menswear. They were called Revolution, East, Uniformity, Body Consciousness, and Courting/Exquisite. I’ll give a short synopsis of each.

Revolution

This gallery showed the evolution from 3 piece suits with multiple layers to less constrained and free moving versions.

East

This gallery covered more international styles. This included African, Asian, and European influence.

This gallery included clothing that had to be mass produced for military and other uses. There were also suits in this gallery as well.

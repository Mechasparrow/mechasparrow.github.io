Going to Write on Medium for 30 Days Straight

I will be writing daily for the next 30 days of August. This will help me build up a new level of consistency and self-discipline.

At the end of this experiment and challenge, I will be writing a post-mortem.

I’m going to use a couple of tools and tactics to pull this off.

Brainstorming

I will allocate 10–15 minutes for brainstorming. This can either take place in the morning or afternoon.

During this brainstorming period, I will use 2 tools in particular. Trello and Mind Mapping.

Trello is a project management tool. It is very versatile and has support for links, attachments, and more.

Mind Mapping is a method to brainstorm thoughts and ideas. It also allows for you better structure your ideas. For more information on mind mapping, I would check out www.mindmapping.com

Planning

To prevent burnout and prevent procrastination. I will work on writing many topics per day. Yet, I will only complete one on a given day.

The rest I can look at the following day and choose to complete. This will prevent me from the fear of a blank page since there will already be words on the page.

I will also incorporate a content creation calendar where I can block out time for specific topics.

Research

I will research about a topic a day before. This will allow me to write accurate and useful articles the following day.

I will also be able to connect ideas from different topics to write new pieces.

Conclusion

This challenge will be an interesting one. Especially with the start of the school just around the corner.

I encourage that you take the challenge with me if you would like to become a better writer.

I can’t wait to see what will happen!

My review of the American Beauty

The movie, American Beauty is quite an interesting story with several plot lines. It slowly tears apart the world of a middle class family.

Each character has their own quirks and motivations.

The Father in the family realizes his life was meaningless and without joy. He begins by quitting his job and shows disregard for his circumstances. He does begin to exercise though.

The wife values her work over her family. She is also selfish and materialistic throughout the story.

The daughter seems to be neglected by her family. She builds up an intimate relationship with the boy next door.

Overall, I believe this film showcases the issues that are not usually seen with family life. It also tells us not to take our life too seriously, for where is the fun in that?

It also has some serious topics. I would recommend that you watch it yourself :)

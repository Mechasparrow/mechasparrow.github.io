I myself know the rigid structure of the public education system due to still attending high school. I also do understand the inability for this to happen. They would not want to give students the ability to question the system that they are currently in. The people running the system don’t want it to change that often.

I do feel that as ideology has replaced philosophy. It has caused to be only see my perspective and stifle creativity. Which is a soft skill that is highly valued I believe.

There are many boundaries to push through that would be almost impossible to attain the public education realm. However, it is good to theorize how education good be done better and have it applied even outside of school.

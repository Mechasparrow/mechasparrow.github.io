Dev log #10 — Podcasts and more Emoji!

Hello everybody. This week I’ve actually been able to work on my side projects. It still has been a jam packed week due to approaching finals and school projects.

Luckily, I’ve gotten past most of them. This week has brought 2 new developments.

I started a podcast and I performed a major update to Emojilog.

The Podcast

My friend and I started a podcast called Hammercircuit. You can check it out at https://soundcloud.com/hammercircuit. We talk about philosophy, life, culture, technology, and business.

Every personal finance class, my friend and I have deep conversations during the class. We also get classwork done, but we also have time for these discussions.

The discussions we have in the class also tend to interest our teacher as well. After a couple weeks of these discussions, we decided that we should start a podcast.

I can’t wait to see how our podcast will turn out.

Emojilog

After more development, I was able to add 2 new features to Emojilog. The features that I added this time around were

  • CSV export
  • Emoji chart

The csv export feature allows for the emoji journal entries to be converted to a csv file. You can then pull that csv file into excel or any other spreadsheet program

The emoji chart generates a graphical representation of the emoji usage.

Yo. Today was that special moon + sun event. I think they call it a solar eclipse. I’m a bit of a science geek sometimes.

Let’s talk about a bit.

How it went?

Yeah. How did it go? Well it lasted about 30 seconds where I was at. I mean 30 seconds of full totality. Everything after that, we had to wear those awesome solar glasses.

Was it memorable?

A little. I know how the sun looks when it does not kill my eyes like any other regular day.

How does it work?

The moon covers the sun. The moon casts a shadow over the earth and makes it look super cool.
